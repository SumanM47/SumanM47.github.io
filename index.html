CV

I am Suman Majumder, a postdoctoral research fellow at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health under the supervision of Drs. Kyu Ha Lee, Jacqueline Starr and Brent Coull. I received my PhD degree in Statistics in 2020 under the direction of Dr. Brian J. Reich of North Carolina State University. I received my bachelors and masters degree from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata (ISI) in India in 2014 and 2016, respectively.

My research interests include :

  • Spatial/Spatiotemporal modeling
  • Computational methods for large datasets
  • Environmental applications
  • High-dimensional data analysis
  • Microbiome data analysis
  • Robust statistical methods
